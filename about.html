About

RLFXO

RLFXO is an independent software studio focused on building thoughtful productivity tools. We believe great software should be simple, private, and respectful of your time.

Our first product, BOLX, is a timeboxing planner for iOS inspired by the time management technique used by some of the world's most productive people. It helps you plan your day in structured time blocks, track your progress, and understand how you spend your hours.

Our Principles

Privacy first. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays on your device and in your own iCloud account.

Quality over quantity. We'd rather build one excellent app than ten mediocre ones.

Accessible by design. Core features are free. No subscriptions, no paywalls on essential functionality.
